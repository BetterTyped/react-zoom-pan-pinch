# Image annotations

An **image labeling tool** built on the library: pan and zoom the photo, then
draw **bounding boxes** (Rectangle mode) or drop **numbered pins** (Pin mode)
directly on the image — even while zoomed in.

In **Rectangle** mode, click and drag to draw a bounding box for object
detection. In **Pin** mode, click to place a point marker. After placing, fill
in a **title**, **description**, and choose a **label category** with
color-coded presets. Click any annotation on the canvas to see its full details
in a popup.

Coordinates are converted from **viewport space** to **content space** using the
live transform state, so markers stay glued to the image at all zoom levels.
**Focus** in the sidebar calls **`zoomToElement`** on the annotation.
