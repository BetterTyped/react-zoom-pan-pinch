# Zoomed Out Content

Start the viewport at a **reduced scale** so users see the full picture at a
glance and can zoom in to explore details. This pattern works well for large
diagrams, floor plans, and high-resolution photos where an overview-first
experience is more useful than starting at 1:1.

Set `initialScale` below `1` to begin zoomed out, and `minScale` to control the
lower bound. The library's `centerOnInit` ensures the content is centered in the
viewport at load time.
