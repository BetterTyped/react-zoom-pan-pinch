# Product Card

A realistic **food delivery product card** with an interactive image zoom viewer.
Scroll, pinch, or double-click the product image to zoom in and see
the juicy meat, fresh vegetables, and sauce details up close. The card includes
sauce/portion selectors, pricing, and an order flow — a common real-world
use case for `react-zoom-pan-pinch`.
