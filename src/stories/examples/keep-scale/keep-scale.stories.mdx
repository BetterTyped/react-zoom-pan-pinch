# Keep Scale

The `KeepScale` component wraps child elements so they **maintain their visual
size** regardless of the current zoom level. As you zoom into the map, the
pin markers stay the same size on screen while the terrain scales normally.

Click any **marker on the map** or a **location card** below to zoom in.
The pins remain crisp and legible at every zoom level — even at 100×.
